Initiatives to Assure
The Kyoto City KYOCERA Museum of Art's Future

The Kyoto City KYOCERA Museum of Art renewal project was realized thanks to the support of the Kyocera Corporation through a
naming rights agreement. To assure the continued operations and the legacy of the Museum, we are expanding initiatives to
individuals and corporations for donations and support. The Museum's collection contains more than 3,600 works, and many of
them are gifts from artists and collectors. We are pleased to receive inquiries on donating artworks from artists, collectors and
owners who wish to share their precious cultural properties with others through the Museum.
Speciﬁc program details at the time of the Renewal Opening will be forthcoming later. We look forward to your support.
